# Economics

Assumption: a high-savings startup buying discounted AI credits through Credex could be worth $1,000–$5,000 gross profit over the relationship depending on credit volume and margin. If average gross profit per converted customer is $2,000, the tool can work with modest conversion.

Funnel: 1,000 visitors → 300 completed audits → 90 email captures → 20 high-savings leads → 8 consultations → 2 purchases. At $2,000 gross profit each, that is $4,000 gross profit from mostly organic acquisition.

CAC: founder DMs and community posts are time-cost heavy but cash-light. If 10 hours produces 100 users and the builder time is valued at $25/hour, CAC per audit is $2.50 and CAC per purchase might be $125 in early manual mode.

To reach $1M ARR in 18 months, Credex would need the tool to become a repeatable lead engine: about 500 qualified audits/month, 50 consultations/month, 10 purchases/month, and roughly $8,333 ARR per customer. The key truth: this only works if the audit identifies real, painful spend above $500/month.
