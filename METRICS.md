# Metrics

North Star metric: qualified savings audits completed. This matches the product goal better than DAU because teams do not audit AI spend daily; they audit when cost pain appears.

Input metrics: completed audit rate, email capture rate after results, and high-savings consultation booking rate. These show whether the landing page, result value, and Credex CTA are working.

Instrument first: landing page view, form start, tool added, audit generated, email captured, share link copied, consultation clicked, and public report viewed.

Pivot trigger: if 100 completed audits produce fewer than 5 high-savings leads or fewer than 15 email captures, the positioning or target audience is wrong.
